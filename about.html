About Me – Randell G.

I am Randell G., a proud Criminology graduate from the University of the Cordilleras. I enjoy balancing both active and reflective activities in life.

I'm passionate about playing basketball it keeps me energized, competitive, and motivated. When I want to relax, I dive into reading Japanese Manga as well as Korean and Chinese Manhwa. These stories bring imagination and emotional depth to my downtime.

One cultural practice I hold dear is chewing betel nut. It's more than a habit it’s part of our identity and tradition in Bontoc, Mountain Province where I come from. It connects me to my roots and the elders of our community.

I also love peaceful night walks. The stillness of the night clears my mind and brings a sense of calm I don’t often find during the day.
