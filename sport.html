My Love for Basketball

Basketball has always been more than just a sport to me—it's a passion, a way to stay active, and a space where I feel truly alive. Every time I step on the court, whether it’s a street game with friends or a local tournament, I feel the rush of energy and joy.

I started playing basketball when I was young, dribbling around on a rough court with makeshift hoops. From there, I grew more confident with my skills—shooting, passing, rebounding—and I fell in love with the fast pace and team spirit that basketball brings.

Why Basketball?

Basketball isn't just about scoring. It's about communication, reading the game, and pushing yourself every single play. I love how it challenges both the body and the mind. It teaches discipline, respect, and resilience—traits that I carry with me even off the court.

Whether I'm shooting hoops solo to clear my head or competing with teammates in a tight match, basketball has been a constant in my life. It's more than a hobby—it's a part of who I am.

Where I Play

I usually play in our local barangay court here in Bontoc, and sometimes in nearby schools or sports events. Win or lose, the adrenaline and camaraderie always make it worthwhile. I also follow the NBA and admire players who inspire me with their dedication and skill.

As long as I can run, jump, and shoot, I know basketball will always have a place in my heart.
